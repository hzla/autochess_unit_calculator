How to Use

This webapp calculates your chances of finding a unit given your courier level, the unit cost, and the number of units bought out. Additionally if you want to see your chances of hitting a unit given a certain amount of gold, you can fill in the gold to spend parameter.

You can view your chances of finding more than one unique unit at a time by clicking the green Add button. Just fill in the unit cost in the field that pops up. A corresponding field for the number of units bought out will appear directly underneath. In this case, the table will show your chances of hitting any of the units for each reroll.

Note that this calculator is not 100% accurate due to not factoring in all the units that have been bought out by all the players.

You can see more information about unit chances here in the AutoChess Wiki.
